Intrinsics wrapped for SVE256

Notations are as follows:

  • T for trick usually using other intrinsics

  • E for scalar emulation

  • NOOP for no operation

  • NA means the operator does not exist for the given type

  • intrinsic for the actual wrapped intrinsic
